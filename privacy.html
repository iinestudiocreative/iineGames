Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

iineGames / iineStudio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the EasyTrip application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name (username)
  • Profile picture (if provided via Google OAuth)
  • Authentication credentials (handled securely by Firebase Authentication)

Trip & Usage Data

When you use the App, we collect:

  • Itineraries and trip details you create (destinations, stops, notes)
  • Shared trip data and collaboration information
  • App usage analytics (pages visited, features used)

Device Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • Enable trip planning, sharing, and collaboration features
  • Synchronize your data across devices
  • Improve and optimize the App's performance and user experience
  • Communicate with you regarding updates or support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication). We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Security Rules to restrict unauthorized access
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Auth (email/password and Google OAuth)

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your data may be shared in the following cases:

Shared with Purpose
Other EasyTrip users When you share a trip or collaborate on an itinerary
Google Firebase Data storage, authentication, and analytics
Legal authorities If required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, hosting
  • Google Play Services — App distribution
  • Google Maps Platform — Maps and location services

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the App
  • Modify your account information at any time
  • Delete your account and associated data through the App settings
  • Export your trip data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the App

To exercise any of these rights, you can contact us at support@iinestudio.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for statistical purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Cookies & Local Storage

The web version of the App may use local storage and cookies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Store your language preferences
  • Improve performance through caching

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.
